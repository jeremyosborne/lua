Expectations and Assumptions

  • Content level: beginning to intermediate.
  • The content is technical and assumes you are familiar with basic programming concepts or are willing to learn them.
  • This course starts off with the basics and moves through more involved examples.
  • A lot of the power in Lua comes from features not found in some mainstream languages.

Agenda

  • Lua core language, datatypes, and constructs
  • Working with the environment: what you can and can't do
  • Tables: the flexible, do everything object
  • OOP in Lua, or how I stopped worrying and loved to learn the metaclass
  • Esoterics: protected calls, coroutines, sandboxing
  • Lua usage in games: code, and more code, along with thought and discussion on how best to make use of it
